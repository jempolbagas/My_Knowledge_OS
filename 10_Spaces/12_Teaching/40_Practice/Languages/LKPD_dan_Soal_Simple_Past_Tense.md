## BAGIAN III: Kunci Jawaban, Pembahasan, & Rubrik Penilaian

### 🔑 Kunci Jawaban LKPD

#### Aktivitas 1: Matrix Classification
1. *Study* ➔ **Studied** (Regular) ➔ Belajar
2. *Buy* ➔ **Bought** (Irregular) ➔ Membeli
3. *Stop* ➔ **Stopped** (Regular) ➔ Berhenti
4. *Go* ➔ **Went** (Irregular) ➔ Pergi
5. *Cry* ➔ **Cried** (Regular) ➔ Menangis
6. *Read* ➔ **Read** (Irregular) ➔ Membaca
7. *Visit* ➔ **Visited** (Regular) ➔ Mengunjungi
8. *Make* ➔ **Made** (Irregular) ➔ Membuat
9. *Dance* ➔ **Danced** (Regular) ➔ Menari
10. *Swim* (V1) ➔ **Swam** (Irregular) ➔ Berenang

#### Aktivitas 2: Comparative Matrix
1. **(-)** *Tasya did not (didn't) sing beautifully on the competition yesterday.*  
   **(?)** *Did Tasya sing beautifully on the competition yesterday?*
2. **(-)** *The students were not (weren't) very excited during the museum tour.*  
   **(?)** *Were the students very excited during the museum tour?*
3. **Transformation:** *Bagus was sick two days ago.*

#### Aktivitas 3: Story Detective
1. **go ➔ went:** Subjek *Rian and his family* menceritakan masa lalu, membutuhkan Verb 2 (*went*).
2. **was ➔ were:** Subjek *They* adalah bentuk jamak (*plural*), sehingga To Be yang tepat adalah *were*.
3. **swimmed ➔ swam:** *Swim* adalah *irregular verb*, bentuk V2-nya adalah *swam* (bukan *swimmed*).
4. **builded ➔ built:** *Build* adalah *irregular verb*, bentuk V2-nya adalah *built* (bukan *builded*).
5. **didn't brought ➔ didn't bring:** Setelah *didn't*, kata kerja harus kembali ke bentuk dasar / Verb 1 (*bring*).

 ---

### 🔑 Kunci Jawaban Soal Evaluasi Mandiri

#### A. Pilihan Ganda
1. **C (see):** Kalimat tanya verbal (*Did + Subject + Verb 1*).
2. **C (read):** Kata kerja *read* adalah irregular verb dengan bentuk V1=V2=V3 (*read* dibaca "red").
3. **B (were):** Subjek *Jungkook and his dancer friends* bersifat jamak (*plural noun*), kalimat nominal bentuk negatif butuh *were not*.
4. **B (didn't):** Kalimat verbal negatif (*Subject + didn't + Verb 1*).
5. **B (Soekarno was the first president of Indonesia):** Pilihan B adalah kalimat nominal yang tepat. (A salah karena *didn't went*, C salah karena *did ... enjoyed*, D salah karena *were visited*).

#### B. Uraian Penalaran
1. **Perbaikan:** `"My father didn't answer my phone call because he was driving his car (atau: he drove his car)."`  
   **Pembahasan:** 
   - *didn't answered* harus diubah menjadi *didn't answer* karena setelah *didn't* harus menggunakan Verb 1.
   - *was drove* salah susunan; harus menjadi past continuous (*was driving*) atau simple past (*drove*).
2. **Rubrik Esai:**  
   - Menggunakan 2 kalimat verbal (V2/did) + 1 kalimat nominal (was/were) = 50%  
   - Menggunakan Time Signal lampau yang tepat = 25%  
   - Kelancaran narasi & tata bahasa = 25%
