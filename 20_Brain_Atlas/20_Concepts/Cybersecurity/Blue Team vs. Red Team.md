## The idea (one clear statement)
Cybersecurity operations are often divided into two contrasting yet complementary functions: **Red Teaming** (offensive security, simulating adversary tactics and exploiting vulnerabilities) and **Blue Teaming** (defensive security, monitoring systems, detecting attacks, and responding to incidents).

## Why it matters / how it connects
The interaction between the two teams drives continuous improvement in an organization's security posture:
*   **Red Team:** Acts as the attacker. Uses penetration testing, social engineering, and evasion to find weak points.
*   **Blue Team:** Acts as the defender. Uses SIEM logging, endpoint detection (EDR), threat hunting, and incident response to protect assets.
*   **Purple Teaming:** A collaborative approach where both teams share real-time feedback to maximize defensive detection and response capabilities.
