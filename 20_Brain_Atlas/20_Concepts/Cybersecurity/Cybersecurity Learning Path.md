## The idea (one clear statement)
A structured cybersecurity learning path divides education into foundational computer science/IT concepts, general security principles, core technical skills, and deep offensive/defensive specializations.

## Why it matters / how it connects
Failing to establish computer and networking fundamentals (e.g., TCP/IP, operating systems, Linux administration, and programming) makes it extremely difficult to understand or execute advanced security operations like penetration testing, malware analysis, or incident response. This roadmap provides a scaffolding that structures learning and avoids information overload.
